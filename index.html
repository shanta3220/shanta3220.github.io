Hi There!

My name is Nusrat Jahan Shanta, a Game Programmer and Developer originally from Bangladesh, now permanently residing in Canada. With over four years of experience in game development, primarily using Unity3D, I am deeply passionate about the industry. My enthusiasm for gaming naturally led me to pursue this career. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering, as well as a Master of Science in Management Information Systems. While the constant learning in this field can be challenging, it brings me immense satisfaction and fulfillment.

Riko: The Adventurer

Riko: The Adventurer" is a 2D top-down RPG shooting game developed in Unity3D, where the main character strives to become a 7th-grade adventurer by gaining experience. I presented this game as my final year project for my Bachelor of Science degree. It is currently still in development.

LSD: Last Survival Days

LSD: Last Survival Days" is a top-down survival shooting game where players can choose from one of four members of LSD. This action-packed game features the band’s debut album as the soundtrack, all while battling to save the world from the forces of evil.

AR Bornoporichoy

AR Bornoporichoy is an augmented reality app developed in Unity3D designed to help children learn the Bengali alphabet. The app features all Bengali letters, with each letter paired with an object whose name begins with that letter, along with audio for identification.
